Our Story

Y&Q was born from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could make electric bikes that don't compromise on performance, style, or sustainability?

Founded in 2019, our team of engineers, designers, and cycling enthusiasts came together with a shared passion for creating the perfect electric bike. We started with a vision to build bikes that would not only help people get around more efficiently but also contribute to a cleaner, healthier planet.

Today, Y&Q has grown into a trusted name in the electric bike industry, known for our innovative designs, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to quality. Our bikes are designed and engineered to handle everything from daily commutes to weekend adventures, from city streets to mountain trails.

We're proud to be part of the electric mobility revolution, and we're excited to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible with electric bikes.
